Master of Business Administration with specialization in General Management


Location
by correspondence or ESG Douala
  • Continuing training
  • Program by correspondence

The Master of Business Administration with specialization in General Management can be taught in several ways: face-to-face or by correspondence (100% e-learning) for employees in continuing education.

The aim of this training program is to enable students to acquire a double competence.
Students without a background in management studies are made aware of the common features and specificities of different types of organizations that can host them (SMEs or multinationals, service companies or industrial companies).
Emphasis is on general knowledge of management (management, marketing, finance) and on the mastery of ICT (information and communication technologies).
The versatility of the diploma and its strong professional anchoring are assets for rapid professional integration into management positions and for a greater dynamic in career development.

2 compulsory internship periods of 3 to 6 months complete the training.

The objectives of the Master’s in Business Administration in e-learning

  • Give a new impetus to your career and move towards a more strategic dimension, without interrupting your professional activity.
  • Continue your studies in a Master’s program.
  • Acquire a double competence.

The advantages of the Master in Business Administration in e-learning

Non-managers are made aware of the common features and specificities of different types of organizations that can host them (for example: SMEs or multinational, service companies or industrial companies).

Emphasis is placed on general knowledge of management (management, marketing, finance) and on the mastery of NICT (new information and communication technologies).
The versatility of the diploma and its strong professional anchoring are assets for rapid professional integration in management positions and for a more dynamic career development.

Former participant’s testimony

With the Master of Business Administration, the emphasis is on general knowledge in management (management, marketing, finance) but also on the mastery of ICT. Working and studying at a distance thus involves a combination of synchronous and asynchronous work in groups and the obligatory use of Internet tools to communicate and meet one another.

Silvain Marvié
